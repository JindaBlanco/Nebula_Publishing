Our Story

Art that breathes with you

Nebula Publishing creates premium digital coloring books designed for adults who seek calm, creativity, and connection. Each illustration is meticulously hand-crafted with intricate detail — a sanctuary from the noise of everyday life.

Based in Zambia and publishing globally, we believe that art is not a luxury — it is a necessity. Our collections draw from nature, cosmos, and culture to create experiences that are both meditative and magnificent.

Every Nebula book is available as an instant digital download, printable at home on any paper you love.
